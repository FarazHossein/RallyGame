Mahmoud Rally 8 -Supreme- is a time-trial racing game.
Your goal is to race around the track in the least amount of time.
In order to win the game, you must finish faster than the current best time. If you fail, you lose.
You must follow the track as there are checkpoints. If you go off the track, you will slow down.
W - Accelerate
S - Brake/Reverse
A - Steer Left
D - Steer Right
